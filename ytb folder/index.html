About This Website

    
This website is your portal to explore the solar system and beyond. Learn fun facts, view real images, and test your space knowledge.

  


  

    
    

      
      
Mercury

      
The smallest planet and closest to the Sun.

    

    

      
      
Venus

      
Venus is extremely hot and has a thick atmosphere.

    

    

      
      
Earth

      
Our home planet, the only one known to support life.

    

    

      
      
Mars

      
Known as the Red Planet, Mars has the largest volcano in the solar system.

    

    

      
      
Jupiter

      
Jupiter is the largest planet and has a massive storm called the Great Red Spot.

    

    

      
      
Saturn

      
Saturn is famous for its beautiful ring system.

    

    

      
      
Uranus

      
Uranus rotates on its side and has faint rings.

    

    

      
      
Neptune

      
Neptune has strong winds and storms in its atmosphere.
